Help & FAQs

Shortcut Details
? Press '?' from you keyboard to open and close help box.
F2 By pressing 'F2' key from function keys or you can 'click' to activate the editor then you can start typeing. When you press a button after activating the editor timer will start counting immediately.
ESC You can press 'ESC' key to close help box or you can just click on the cross button on top right corner. Also You can press 'ESC' key from your keyboard to deactivate the editor. By deactivating editor timer will stop counting. To clear you will need to reset the editor.
Ctrl + Enter You can reset the editor by pressing 'Ctrl' + 'Enter' key from your keyboard.
Ctrl + Shift + S You can press the 'S' key by pressing and holding down the Ctrl key and the Shift key from your keyboard to open the setting panel.
↑ ↓ You can press '↑' Up Arrow key from your keyboard to enable sound and increase volume by a factor of 10 out of 100 and also press '↓' Down Arrow key to decrease volume by a factor of 10 out of 100. When volume is '0' the sound will be disabled.
Ctrl + Alt + C You can press and hold Control key and Alt key and then press 'C' to enable and disable "Stop the cursor when error happen" setting.
